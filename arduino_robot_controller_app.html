Joystick mode sends single ASCII characters: F forward, B backward, L left, R right, S stop.

Voice mode sends: ^ forward, - backward, < left, > right, * stop.

Baud rate: 9600. Pair your HC-05/HC-06 in your phone's Bluetooth settings before connecting here.
